Investor Provision

DESCRIPTION

At LGC we also have provisions for investors! At the point of investing in LGC, a potion of land of equivalent value will instantly be allocated to our investor(s) and at the end of their investment tenure, their capital is returned in full. Please note: The allocated land is still their, but if they no longer want the said property then a return on investment (ROI) of nothing less than 45 percent will be given to them instantly alongside their initial capital. In a situatuion where our clients (you) desire to sell off their properties within our estate, we are also available to do that on your behalf. Thanks as we look foward to making wealth together.
